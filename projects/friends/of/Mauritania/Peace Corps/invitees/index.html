Info for Invitees to Mauritania

Salutations if you are a new invitee to the Peace Corps in Mauritania. This page is here to provide you with a bit of introduction and information you might find useful in your preparations.

Listserv

There are two forums for invitees: a group begun in 2005 and another group formed while the intial group was overrun by porm spammers. You'll likely be able to find any answers you're looking for in one of those places.

Webpage

A webpage is slowly taking form for the volunteers from Mauritania. One of the most interesting things to people soon to be joining us are the links which include several sets of pictures and also journals. There is also a link to the original webpage that is in the process of being revised, but which has an abundance of information.

Stretching

You'll spend a good part of your first few months here stretching your legs. (Literally, Mauritanian toilet = hole in the ground.) Since at times you may be doing so hurredly you may like to start getting ready with some yoga. The moon salutation is a good one to get the hamstrings and quadriceps.
