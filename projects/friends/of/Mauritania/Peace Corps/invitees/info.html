Mauritania Volunteer Info

The volunteers in Mauritania have a list of interesting information that is used to generate the roster, map and staff guide. Certain information (site assignments, contact information) is carefully guarded by the Peace Corps and therefore protected by a password. In the pursuit of helping to acclimate new trainees, this information can be made available to them assuming that they are willing to enter their own information.

So, if you are an invitee or a current or past volunteer that would like to update their informaiton, fill out this form. If you are not currently in the rolls then I will send a password to access them to the email address you supply. If you have any questions feel free to contact me.
