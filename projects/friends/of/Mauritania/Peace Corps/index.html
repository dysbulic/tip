Peace Corps Mauritania

Bienvenue and مرحبًا

This page is a home on the web for Peace Corps (PC) volunteers serving in (or who will or have served) in Mauritania (Republic Islamique de Mauritanie (RIM)). Things are coming together slowly but surely. In the interim if you need anything, email me.

Links

I am slowly accruing a list of PC-RIM links.

Cookbook

Once upon a time there was a cookbook for PC-RIM. The electronic copy suffered an unfortunate disappearance leaving only a few paper copies scattered around the country. I got one and scanned all the pages. There is a big png of each and a little jpeg for quick download. It'll make it back into electronic form eventually, but for now this is it.

Nouakchott Notes

Nouakchott Notes is the title of our quarterly newsletter. I found the last couple.

Original Site

There is a very complete site that was developed by a previous volunteer and which is currently under revision. Some of the information is slightly out of date, but it is an excellent resource.

Mauritania Map

I am in the process of playing with a map. This one shows various cities by GPS coordinates and puts them on a map. If you have already put in a password to access the contact list it will show which volunteers are in each city. You have to have a SVG viewer for it to work.
