Because accounts will be shared and need to stay standardized, users will have a minimum of access to the system settings. That part of the setup is detailed at: http://odin.himinbi.org/lock_down_registry.html.

By far the most difficult aspect to getting this setup working are the multilanguage issues. For companies to begin to support other romance languages such as French is relatively new. Support for Arabic is generally very poor. My ability to research the subject is very limited as well and I am forced to rely solely on the experience of others which is proving not especially productive. To be complete, this setup needs, at the least, an Arabic word processor. That would complete the big three of operating system, word processor and web browser.
