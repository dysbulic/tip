Friends of Mauritania Special Projects

1999 brought new interests and goals to FORIM. As you can see in What is FORIM?, FORIM has decided to become an organized supporter for the improvement of Mauritania. FORIM is currently rallying membership support to develop specific goals and guidelines for developmental and educational projects. If you are interested in being a virtual member of one of the working groups, contact us at friends@forim.org.
