Recipe for Love Cake

2  Laughing Eyes

2  Loving Arms

2  Well Shaped Legs

2  Firm Milk Containers

1  Fur lined Mixing Bowl

2  Large Nuts

1  Large Banana

Directions

1.  Look into Laughing Eyes

2.  Fold in Loving Arms

3.  Spread Well Shaped Legs

4.  Squeeze and Massage Milk Containers until Fur Lined Mixing Bowl is
Well greased. Check Frequently with Middle Finger.

5.  Add Banana - Work in and out until well Creamed

6.  Cover with Nuts and Sigh Relief

Cake done when banana become soft. Be sure to wash mixing utensils
 and don't lick the bowl.

If cake begins to rise leave town immediately.
