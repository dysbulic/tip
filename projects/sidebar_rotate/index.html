Sidebar Rotation

MPP wants a sidebar with sections whose content changes periodically. I figure this ought to be pretty since the page is not terribly currently.

I have seen other CSS crossfaders at brand spanking new and mike-o-matic, but they weren't exactly what I needed. Specifically, the behaviors that I want are:

  • sensitive to mouse hover
    • will not change while someone is hovered over the element
    • if someone moves their mouse over while fading out, the fade will reverse

It annoys me when I see something and want to click on it, but can't because it fades out too quickly.

Animals

Penguin

One of about 40 species of flightless birds. The 120km journey of the emperor penguin to mate was the subject of a 2005 film: March of the Penguins. Penguins have no external genitalia. Chromosome testing must be done in order to determine a penguin's sex.

Lion

Second only to the tiger in size, lions may cross breed with tigers to produce ligers or tigons.

Scorpion

Scorpion courtship consists of a promenade á deux where the male lays a spermatophore and guides the female over it to inseminate her. After a year long viviparous gestation period, the scorplings are born. Scorpions fluoresce green under a blacklight.
