Sunflower Oil

Sunflower oil is not only a healthy and delicious option for all your cooking needs, it also serves a variety of unexected uses around the house:

  • Hinges: Tired of the youngins being able to track your nocturnal activities? A liberal coat of sunflower oil to the underside of the bed will provide you all the privacy you could hope for. Rumord by the Aztecs to be a potent aphrodesiac, it will also add a hearty scent to the room certain to set the proper mood.
  • Slippin' 'n' Slidin': There's nothing like family fun in the sun on the Slip'n'Slide™, but are you ready to take it to the next level? Coating yourself with sunflower oil will guarantee a slide the likes of which you have never experienced.
  • First Aid: Sunflower seed oil is a natural coagulant and excellent for closing small cuts. For example the thousands you will suffer when your well greased body shoots off the end of the Slip'n'Slide™ at 50mph and into the grass.
