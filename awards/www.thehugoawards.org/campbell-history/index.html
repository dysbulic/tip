Astounding Award

The Astounding Award for Best New Writer is currently administered by WSFS and the results are announced at the Hugo Award ceremony. However, the Astounding is awarded by Dell Magazines, not by WSFS. Historical data about the Astounding Award (renamed from the John W. Campbell Award as of the 2020 award year) is presented here as part of of WSFS’s duty to Dell Magazines regarding administration of the award.

*Finalists in their 2nd year of eligibility (when known).

No Award: In some years, the members voted to place No Award higher than some of the finalists. When this has happened, we list No Award as a finalist in the order that it places. If No Award is not listed, it means that it placed lower than all of the finalists.
