There is no Board of Directors, no small group who makes the decision. All decisions about changes to the Hugo Awards must originate from the members and be discussed and approved by the vote of the members of two consecutive Worldcons. If you are a member of next year’s Worldcon, you can propose such changes and attempt to convince the other members to approve your proposals.

When it comes to the Hugo Awards, there really isn’t a faceless “them” who decides how the awards should work; there is only “us,” and anyone can become “one of us.”
