I woke up this morning and started to write and couldn't rememeber what 
day number it was, so I wrote a little program to help me remember. Then I 
added this to my bashrc:

alias days="echo You have been here \$(~/days.pl 2003/6/24 \$(date +%Y/%m/%d)) days and have \$(~/days.pl \$(date +%Y/%m/%d) 2005/9/16) left"

So when I type days it says "You have been here 21 days and have 794 
left". 794. Damnation that's alot of days.

I include the program in case someone happens to read this some day in a 
similar situation.
