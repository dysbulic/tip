Mímisbrunnr (Mímis)

Mímis is a project to create a publish/subscribe network for the efficient distribution of cryprographically-signed XML documents. The system is designed to serve as a data-sharing mechanism for an open-data-backed content management system. It is the basis of Will Holcomb's Master's thesis and one of the underlying technologies in the Department of Happiness five-year plan. As of Jan. 2009, the project is in a research phase.
