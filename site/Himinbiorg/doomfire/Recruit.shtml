Temporary Recruitment Policy

Right now if you want to join up hop over to the forums and introduce yourself.

This is how we are going to handle recruitment for the time being since the game hasn't been released yet. We will let whoever wants to join in as Grunts. If we get to know you better in the interim or if you exhibit a lot of initiative we may find it appropriate to promote you to a more prestigious position. I'm not going to ask anyone to choose their speciality before the game is actually release. Also know that as a grunt your position in clan is in no way certain, you will either have to prove your worth and be promoted or get kicked out altogether.
