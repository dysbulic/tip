Doomfire Clan Organization

It should be noted that everything here is tentative, the game not having been released yet. =)

We specifically designed our organization scheme to allow everyone the potential to advance in rank. The primary clan will consist only of the Warchief and the Doomfire Council, which will have one representative from each of the subgroups listed below.

Subguilds

Each of the subgroups shall have 5 leaders who have proven themselves elite within their area of expertise. In addition to these a number one ranked person from each subgroup shall have a seat on the Doomfire Council and will represent their speciality to the clan as a whole. This player's spot on the Council can only be challenged by one of the 5 elite members of that player's subgroup, who would have to defeat the council member 3 out of 4 battles. The specializations are:

  • Infantry: Melee Types
  • Havoc Priests: Offensive Spell Casters
  • Healers: Healers and Buffers
  • Cavalry: Any Mounted Specialists
  • Sneaks: Stealthy Types
  • Bombarders: Ranged Melee (archers)
  • Veterans: Defeated or Retired Warchiefs and Council Members
  • Crafters: Crafty Folk... Challenging rank will not involve regular combat
  • Mahirim: ...will only be formed when enough Mahirim have joined...
  • Grunts: Trial Members without specialization, NO Council Representation

As you can see the council can potentially have up to ten members, a council representative for each of the subgroups and the Warchief. It should be noted that the Warchief can only be challenged by one of the existing Council Members. For a council member to take the Warchief's position he must first have the unanimous vote of the council for permission to challenge the Warchief, once that is achieved he must then beat the Warchief 3 battles consecutively. If he fails noone may rechallenge the Warchief for 3 months. This will ensure the Warchief's position rarely changes.

Dueling for Rank

Challenging isn't a terribly complicated process. If I am an Infantry regular, the only person I can challenge is one of the Infantry Leaders, and I will have to beat the leader two out of three times to claim the Infantry Leadership position. The person defending their rank gets to choose the time and place of the duel, and they are only obligated to defend their rank once per week, though they may take on as many challengers as they like. If they only challenge one person per week then they should duel the Challenger that most agree is best fit to face the Defender in combat.

All challenges must be witnessed by at least one Council Member or the Warchief and 5 other regular members to ensure fairness. No looting of items is allowed and it must all be honourable with no trash talking. Leaders are strong and honourable not smack talkers.

The Parts Form the Whole

Everyone will pay a tax to the main Doomfire Clan which will be used to build our main city and all cities of the subgroups. The main city will be built first then each speciality specific area will be built later. The subgroups will have to accept input from the Council on how and where to build their cities, so as to maximize defensive capabilites and to avoid sloppy cities.
