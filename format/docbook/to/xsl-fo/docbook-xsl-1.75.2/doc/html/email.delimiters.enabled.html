email.delimiters.enabled

email.delimiters.enabled — Generate delimiters around email addresses?

Synopsis

<xsl:param name="email.delimiters.enabled" select="1"></xsl:param>

Description

If non-zero, delimiters [1] are generated around e-mail addresses (the output of the email element).


[1] For delimiters, the stylesheets are currently hard-coded to output angle brackets.
